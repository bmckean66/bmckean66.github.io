What are Scrattle Bugs?

Scrattle Bugs are the pesky bugs shown in the picture above. Known by the scientific name Scrattlys Halys, they have recently become a major pest in the Pacific Northwest, and throughout the western United States. Though not poisonous, their bites are very painful and cause significant swelling. Some also have severe allergic reactions to Scrattle Bug bites, causing migraines, elephantiasis of body parts, minor to major paralysis, or spontaneous combustion.

About Us

Scrattle Technology Inc.

We at Scrattle Technology Inc. are committed to making high quality and sustainable products to help keep your home pest-free. Our company started just two years ago as the dream of one ambitious young man, and has now grown into the one of the largest producers of pest control devices in Oregon. Scrattle Technology Inc. currently produces 3 products, the Scrattle Bug Trap, Scrattle-Off Spray, and Scrattle Screen. Our products are now sold in 9 states across the western United States and we are currently looking to expand further.

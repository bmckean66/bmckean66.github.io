About Brian

Brian McKean is a senior from Beaverton, Oregon who is majoring in classical studies with a minor in history. At Willamette University, Brian is very involved and maintains a GPA of 3.74. He is a leader for the Outdoor Program, tutor for Chemawa Indian School of Partnership Program, and a leader in the Steppin' Out program.

Leadership Experience

I have gained substantial leadership experience during my time at Willamette University. I was a leader in the Steppin' Out freshman orientation program for 3 years. I also served as an Outdoor Program leader for 4 years, which had a similar structure to the Steppin' Out program. As a leader, I was responsible for taking groups of incoming freshman on hikes throughout Oregon in order to help them make friends and acclimate to college life. Through this program, I was able to gain experience leading groups of people, facilitating interaction and bonding between new students, and develop my skills in risk management and problem solving.

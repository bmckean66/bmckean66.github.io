FAQ
All of your questions answered right here!

Can the Scrattle Bugs escape from the trap?

No they cannot. The extra-smooth plastic material and Contoured Surface Technology® prevent the Scrattle Bugs from adhering to the walls of the trap and climbing out.

How long does the Trap Last?

The trap itself should last forever! The only thing you will have to do is replace the batteries in the LED light when they run out.

Is there a warranty?

The Scrattle Bug Trap has a lifetime warranty that covers any damage, no questions asked. There is also a money back guarantee if you are not entirely satisfied with your product.

What do I do when the light bulb battery runs out?

The bottom panel of the trap unscrews in order to allow easy access to the light bulb and the ability to empty the trap.

Will the trap work for other insects and pests as well?

Any pests that are attracted to light will be attracted to the Scrattle Bug Trap.

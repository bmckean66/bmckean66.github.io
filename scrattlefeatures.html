Features

NEW Contoured Surface Technology®

Once the scrattle bugs enter the trap, the slippery and curved surfaces prevent the scrattle bugs from adhering to the walls and climbing out.

Sustainability

The Scrattle Technology Inc. team is committed to sustainability. The Scrattle Bug Trap is made from 98% recycled materials and energy efficient LED light bulbs.

Long Lasting LED Light

The trap includes a 1-watt mini-LED light bulb to attract the scrattle bugs. The bulb uses a lithium battery which provides up to 2 years of battery life so you don't have to worry about changing the bulb

How the Trap Works

The Scrattle bugs are attracted to the LED light in the bottom of the trap.

The Scrattle bugs enter the trap.

The Contoured Surface Technology® prevents the Scrattle bugs from escaping the trap.

Your home is now Scrattle bug free!
